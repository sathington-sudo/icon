Latest From The Bench

Identifying the "Click of Death"

Look, we’ve all been there. You hear it. Click. Click. Click. It’s the sound of your data screaming for help. In the tech world, we call that the "Click of Death."

The No-Nonsense Perspective

If your drive is clicking, it is a hardware emergency. Stop using it immediately.

Why is it happening?

It usually boils down to physical trauma, old age, or power issues. Every single click is the mechanical arm potentially scraping against the platters where your data lives.
