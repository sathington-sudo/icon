"For years, I’ve sat across the counter from neighbors who have lost their hard-earned savings to overseas scammers. I’m tired of seeing it. I want to be your First Line of Defense."
— Chris @ Icon Computers

If you see any of the following things happen on your computer or phone, STOP. Do not pay. Do not click. Just call me first for free advice.

The Gift Card Trap

No legitimate company will ever ask you to pay for a service using gift cards from Target, CVS, or Amazon.

The Crypto Demand

If someone tells you to go to a "Bitcoin ATM" to move your money, they are stealing from you. It is gone forever.

The Screen Share

If a stranger asks you to download "AnyDesk" or "TeamViewer" to see your screen, hang up immediately.

The "Refund" Scam

They "accidentally" send too much money and ask for the difference back. It's a trick to drain your account.
